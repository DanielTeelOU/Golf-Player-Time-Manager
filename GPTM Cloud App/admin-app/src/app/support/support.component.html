Registering a new player

Follow the steps below to register a new player

On the home page click on the register tab.

Once there, enter the player's desired login credentials.

Click submit to save. If there is a mistake the account can always be reset at a later date.

If the success message displays then the account has been created.

If not, then reload the page and try again.
